What is Frugal Innovation?

Frugal innovation is all about innovation, collaboration and creativity. We highly value the idea of developing products and solutions with great quality and low cost. Frugal Innovation is an approach to research and development that goes against the traditional way of thinking about innovation. Rather than increasing the complexity of a product, Frugal Innovation aims to make great solutions availible everywhere.

Innovation Camps

We are hosting innovation camps to help you find better solutions. Whether you are a public institution or a private business, our highly competent experts can help you to find the best solutions. Contact us to schedule a meeting.

Solutions that benefit everyone

Our innovation methods aim to make life better for all people. We think there is a great, untapped potential existing in finding solutions that are both societal and capable of producing growth.
